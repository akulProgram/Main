Berin Mini-Pivot

How It Works

A deep dive into the design, setup process, and specifications of the Berin Mini-Pivot irrigation system.

Setup Process

From Box to Irrigation in 4 Steps

01

Anchor the Center

Fix the center pivot post into the ground at the middle of your plot. The anchor base holds the system in place and connects to your water source.

02

Assemble the Arm

Connect the lightweight pipe sections and sprinkler heads along the pivot arm. Sections click together — no welding, no bolts, no tools needed.

03

Connect Water

Attach a standard hose from your water source — borehole pump, river, tank, or gravity-fed system — to the center inlet.

04

Move & Irrigate

Walk the wheeled end around the center point. The sprinkler heads distribute water evenly across the circular coverage area as you go.
