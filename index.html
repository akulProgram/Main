The Challenge

Millions of African farmers depend on rain.
They don't have to.

Traditional lateral-move irrigation systems cost tens of thousands of dollars, require electricity, need specialized installation — and ship as massive, bulky equipment that can't reach most rural areas. Smallholder farms have been locked out. Until now.

Why MvuraFlow

Built for the Real World

Affordable

A fraction of the cost of commercial pivots. Designed so smallholder farmers can access lateral irrigation without large capital investment.

Manual to Motorized

Starts as a manually-moved system — no motors, no fuel, no power grid. When you're ready, bolt on the capstan motor to automate movement. Grow at your own pace.

One Model, Many Crops

Base height of 2.2m covers vegetables and tobacco. Need maize? Bolt on the extension to reach 3.0m. One system that adapts as your farm evolves.

Even Water Coverage

Lateral design delivers uniform irrigation in straight passes across rectangular fields, reducing water waste and ensuring every row gets what it needs.

Ships in a Crate

Each unit packs flat into a single compact crate. 20 units fit in one standard container — no oversized freight, no special trucks, no cranes needed.

Water Efficient

Uses significantly less water than flood or furrow irrigation. Helps conserve scarce water resources in drought-prone regions.
